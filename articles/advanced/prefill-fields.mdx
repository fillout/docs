## How to pre-fill a field

You can pre-fill **Fillout** questions with either static or dynamic data from integrations like Airtable and Notion. Click a field followed by the `settings icon`. In the right-hand panel, click `+` to look for a default value.

![Screenshot2025 02 05at2 47 33PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-02-05at2.47.33PM.png)

## **Pre-fill basics**

You can set a fixed value as the pre-fill by just typing some text under **Default value**.

If you click the blue `+` icon, you can pre-fill the field with:

- [URL Parameters](https://www.notion.so/URL-Parameters-Hidden-fields-f11c3b56de8e4c7d9cccf47825ccf3c4?pvs=21)
- [User information from a Login step](https://www.notion.so/7c96895e91714b839a6556712f1bc49e?pvs=21)
- Answer to another **Fillout** field
