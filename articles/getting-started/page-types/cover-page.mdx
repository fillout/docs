## How to add a cover page

Click `+ Add page` followed by `Cover`. Then, enter the text.

![C1 Web](/images/c1.webp)

When the form is opened, the text will **animate smoothly**. You can also change the **Button text** through the left-hand panel.

## Customize your cover page

Change the **Custom cover page layout** through the left-hand panel. Check out this [guide](https://www.fillout.com/help/layout). You can also add a small **Image above title** through the same panel.

![C2 Web](/images/c2.webp)

Customize the **design** of your page and add a [logo](https://www.notion.so/Add-a-logo-f536f629e0304721a4cc56bbeed4a6ab?pvs=21) by clicking `Theme` in the upper left corner of the page. Learn more [here](https://www.notion.so/Themes-f29afca7098d44eda5f076c16b490868?pvs=21).
