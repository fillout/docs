## Add a new form page

All newly created forms have a **form page** labelled as **Page** where you can add any type of [field](https://www.notion.so/Field-types-1aa7ec8c6ad941c782be2af823ab3459?pvs=21) from the left-hand panel. Simply click or drag & drop your chosen field.

![7https%3a%2f%2fprod Files Secure Web](/images/7https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.webp)

You can add more form pages by clicking `+ Add page` followed by `Form`.
