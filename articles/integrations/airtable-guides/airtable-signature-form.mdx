## Overview

Signature forms are useful for all sorts of use cases, like contract signing, conference check-in forms, waiver forms, and many more. Your can make unlimited forms for free, with 1000 submissions per month. Learn more about e-signatures [here](https://www.notion.so/Add-e-signatures-to-your-form-e00f5182d2ab4d28b8572ac74e7d1a22?pvs=21).

## How to add a signature field

In the left-hand panel of your Fillout form, click or drag and drop the `signature` field.

Once you have the **signature** field added, you will end up with a form that looks like this.
