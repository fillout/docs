## How to p**ull external information into your form**

### **1. Select your integration**

Navigate to the **Integrate** page from the top menu and choose an integration.

### 2. Add a pre-fetch record

Then, click `+ Add new` under **Pre-fetch records**.

![Screenshot2025 02 14at11 33 56AM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-02-14at11.33.56AM.png)

Enter a **name** for your record, then select a **base** and a **table**. Under **Filter by**, choose an Airtable field to match with a [URL parameter](https://www.notion.so/URL-Parameters-Hidden-fields-f11c3b56de8e4c7d9cccf47825ccf3c4?pvs=21) or static text. In this example, we'll select email.

![Screenshot2025 02 14at11 40 20AM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-02-14at11.40.20AM.png)
