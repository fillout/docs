## How to remove the confetti display

By default, when respondents reach the end of their form submission, confetti will be displayed.

![Screenshot2025 02 25at4 35 56PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at4.35.56PM.png)

To disable, click the **Ending** page (bottom of the screen) and then untoggle the `Confetti` switch.

![Screenshot2025 02 25at4 36 44PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at4.36.44PM.png)
