## How to choose a template

First, find the template you would like to clone [here.](https://fillout.com/templates) Then, click `Use template`.

![Template Web](/images/template.webp)

Alternatively, you can click `+ Make a Fillout` on the **homepage** followed by `Templates`.

That’s it\! You now have access to that form on your account. Make any changes you need, [customize it](https://www.notion.so/Styling-Branding-fd24e9d59a8a4e5190d254e5b7b41e00?pvs=21), [connect an integration](https://www.notion.so/Integrations-ff23eebebba04d728aecd4744d814b81?pvs=21), and [publish your form](https://www.notion.so/Sharing-Embedding-40bfa4a36cc142288b123e439abd1f5c?pvs=21) once you’re good to go.

## Make your own template

You can even make your own template from one of your forms and share it inside or outside of your organization. More details [here](https://www.notion.so/Create-your-own-form-template-4aabaa48a5d14ddf8334084c332c1dd9?pvs=21).
