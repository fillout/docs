## How to add a field

Simply click or drag and drop your chosen **field type** from the left-hand panel, then type your question.

![14 Web](/images/14.webp)

Each field is highly customizable. Just click on any field followed by its `settings icon` to open the right-hand panel. Add supporting details, make it required, set up [**conditional logic**](https://www.notion.so/Logic-7bbc1369260d42eb91c081d1463e755d?pvs=21), and more. You can also easily [copy and paste](https://www.notion.so/Copy-and-paste-a-question-069b996a5c5e4f019bc3857c6b9f622d?pvs=21) or [duplicate](https://www.notion.so/Duplicate-existing-questions-808784dbf14a4b7589204020218672e1?pvs=21) your field types.
