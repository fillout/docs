## How to build a form in minutes

### 1. Sign up or log in

Sign up [here](https://app.fillout.com/signup) or log in [here](https://app.fillout.com/login) using any email or your Google account.

### 2. Choose a form type

On your **home** page, click `+ Make a Fillout` , then choose between a **Blank form**, **Template**, or **Connect** with 3rd party app integrations like Airtable, Notion, Hubspot, and more.

![Maf1 Web](/images/maf1.webp)

If you’re unsure about which to use, choose `Blank form`. You can always connect to an [integration](https://www.notion.so/Integrations-ff23eebebba04d728aecd4744d814b81?pvs=21) later.

### 3. Customize the design

Click `Theme` to pick from one of the pre-made themes or [customize your own](https://www.notion.so/Themes-f29afca7098d44eda5f076c16b490868?pvs=21).

![Maf2 Web](/images/maf2.webp)

### 4. Add questions or fields

Click or drag and drop your desired [field types](https://www.notion.so/Field-types-1aa7ec8c6ad941c782be2af823ab3459?pvs=21) from the left-hand panel. Choose between over 40 different types.

![Maf3 Web](/images/maf3.webp)

Then, enter the text and edit it in-line. Click its `settings icon` to customize it in the right-hand panel.

### 5. Publish your form

`Preview` your form to test it. Once everything's good to go, press `Publish`.

![4 Web](/images/4.webp)

That’s it\! Share your **form link** anywhere or **embed your form** on any website. To view form submissions, head over to [**Results**](https://www.notion.so/Results-e59927a7827a4b01baf91a2d8d0afb59?pvs=21) in the top menu.
