## How to view submissions

Open your form, and go to **Results** from the top menu. Sort responses or hide columns by hovering over a column header, followed by clicking `▼` the dropdown icon.

![15 Web](/images/15.webp)

`Filter` responses to narrow responses based on a specific preference. Use `Date range` to set a range for submission/edit dates.

### ⌨️ **Shortcuts**

- `⌘ + F` or `Ctrl + F` while clicking on any cell to search through your entries
- `⌘ + C` or `Ctrl + C` on a cell to copy its contents
- `Double-click any cell` to enlarge its contents (helpful for long inputs)

## View individual submissions

Hover over a cell and click the `expand icon` to view the answers and submission details. Here, you can [comment and tag](https://www.notion.so/Comment-on-form-submissions-1420ee47667b803094add5481be5d26c?pvs=21) your teammates.

![16 Web](/images/16.webp)

## Export submissions

Click the `↓` download icon in the top right to export all of your responses in a formatted CSV respective of the filters.

![17 Web](/images/17.webp)

## Check misconfiguration errors

Fillout alerts you of any misconfiguration errors. For example, you recently changed a field type in your Airtable/Notion database, but forgot to change it in Fillout.

![18 Web](/images/18.webp)

Click `error icon` to view the error message. You can also enable [error notifications](https://www.notion.so/Enable-email-notifications-ea072f172fd140c490551c18cd808c1d?pvs=21) to get notified via email.
