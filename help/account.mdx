## Edit account settings

On the **Home** page, click your `Name` followed by `Settings & Members`. Here, you'll see your team members, invites, account plan, and more.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/account/image-1.png" alt="Account settings" />
</Frame>

## Change account email

If you want to change the email linked to your account, go to `Settings & Members`. Simply click `Manage` next to the role of the particular account.

## Upgrade plan

To upgrade your plan, click **Billing** on the left menu followed by `Change plan`.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/account/image-2.png" alt="Change account email" />
</Frame>

## Key features

### Account Upgrade

Upgrade your account to access advanced **Fillout** features.

### Workspace Management

Create and manage workspaces to organize your forms and collaborate with others. You can create separate [workspaces](/help/workspaces) for different projects or teams.

### Collaboration

[Invite users](/help/invite-collaborators) to collaborate on your forms by granting them access to specific workspaces or forms. [Control their permissions](/help/granular-permissions) and decide whether they can view, edit, [comment on](/help/comment), or manage your forms.
