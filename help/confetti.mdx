## How to remove the confetti display

By default, when respondents reach the end of their form submission, confetti will be displayed.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at4.35.56PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>

To disable, click the **Ending** page (bottom of the screen) and then untoggle the `Confetti` switch.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at4.36.44PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>
