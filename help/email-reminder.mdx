## How to set up

Go to **Notifications** on the left-hand panel. Toggle the `Email reminder` switch. Input the **time before event** when you want the reminder to be sent. Alternatively, you can use our `Event email reminder to invitees` [workflow template](/help/scheduling-workflows#b4ec6e368aca4853afa82cb14846d0cd).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/scheduling/image-35.png" />
</Frame>

Click `Edit email` to format the content of your email. Choose a **Style** and a **Theme**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/scheduling/image-45.png" />
</Frame>

You can reference scheduling details or other form answers by typing `@` or clicking the `+` icon, and choosing the specific **field** or **question**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/scheduling/image-36.png" />
</Frame>
