## How it works

### 1. Switch your form to update mode

Open an existing form. Select `Monday` from the **Integrate** tab in the top menu.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-10at1.45.55PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form" />
</Frame>

Then, click `Update item`.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-10at1.46.22PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form" />
</Frame>

### 2. Use a formula to specify the record to update

In your Monday table, click `+` to add an `Item ID` column. Add another column, and select `Formula` this time. Copy and paste the formula provided on the Fillout integrations page, then click `Set formula`.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-10at1.47.12PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form" />
</Frame>

You'll now get a formula that defines a **unique link** for every item in your table.

### 3. Publish and share your form

That's it\! Click `Update` on the upper right corner. Then, `Publish` your form and **share** the link.

Your form is automatically pre-filled for whichever record it is updating. Go to `Preview` and select a **Record to update** on the top right of your form.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-11at12.44.09PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form" />
</Frame>

## Use a custom link to update an item

By default, items get updated by their unique ID. By toggling the `Custom link` switch, you will be able to choose another way to update your columns on Monday with [URL parameters](/help/url-parameters). For example, through Email input.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-11at1.03.13PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form" />
</Frame>

## Specify a condition

You can run your integration only when a specific condition is met. Check out this [guide](/help/conditional-integration).
