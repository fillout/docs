## How to embed forms to your Notion page

### 1. Type /embed

Then, press `enter`.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-12at4.52.26PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form" />
</Frame>

### 2. Paste the link of your form

In your Fillout form, click `Share` from the top menu to copy your **link**. Then, paste this to your Notion page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-12at4.53.59PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form" />
</Frame>

That’s it\! Your form is now embedded in your Notion page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/contact-us.png" alt="Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form" />
</Frame>

<Check>
  **Good to know:** You can also make a **Fillout** form to update your Notion databases. View the guide [here](https://www.fillout.com/notion).
</Check>
