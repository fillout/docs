## Enable duplicate prevention

Navigate to the **Integrate** tab and select `Airtable`. Then, connect to your Airtable account.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-11at4.12.50PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>

<Note>
This feature is available to **Business** and **Enterprise** users. For more details on pricing, click [here](https://www.fillout.com/pricing).
</Note>

Under **Advanced**, enable the `Prevent duplicates?` toggle.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-11at4.13.20PM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>

Choose a field in the dropdown. In this example, we will prevent submissions from getting saved to Airtable if they match another entry in the Product Name field.

Thatâ€™s it\! Your form will no longer create duplicate entries.
