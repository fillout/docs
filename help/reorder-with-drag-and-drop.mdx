## How to reorder pages using drag and drop

Simply drag pages in the bottom toolbar and position them in the order you would like the form pages to be shown.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/guides/image-92.gif" alt="Reorder pages using drag and drop" />
</Frame>

<Note>
  You cannot put any page before a **Cover** page and after an **Ending** page. Reordering is also disabled if you have added [branching](/help/page-logic) logic to your form. To reorder forms with branching logic, see [page logic](/help/page-logic).
</Note>

## How to reorder question or field options

This can be applied to **Multiple choice**, **Dropdown**, **Checkboxes**, and **Picture choice** field types.

**Multiple choice**

Drag the options in the center of the screen to reorder choices.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/guides/image-93.gif" alt="Reorder multiple choice options" />
</Frame>

**Dropdown and multi-select**

Click the `settings icon` to open the right-hand panel. Drag options there to reorder them.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/guides/image-94.gif" alt="Reorder dropdown options" />
</Frame>

**Checkboxes**

Hover towards the right side of the checkbox option you want to reorder then drag the 6 dots to where you want the option to be placed.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/guides/image-95.gif" alt="Reorder checkboxes" />
</Frame>

**Picture choices**

Simply drag the picture of choice you want to move and drop where you want it to be placed.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/guides/image-96.gif" alt="Reorder picture choices" />
</Frame>
