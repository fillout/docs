## How to add a social media link

From the left-hand panel, click `Social media links`. Buttons for Facebook, X, and Instagram will appear by default.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/question-types/image-5.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>

On the right-hand panel, click **Add link** to add URLs for each respective platform. To delete one, hover over a platform and click the `trash icon`. To change the order of how the platforms appear (left to right), drag them using the double vertical ellipsis symbol `⋮⋮` next to each one.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/question-types/image-6.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>

`+ Add social link` to add buttons for other social media pages, public messaging channels like Telegram or Discord, portfolios like Behance, and more.

The right-hand panel is also where you can edit the color and style of your buttons.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/question-types/image-7.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>
