## How to change your form's theme

On your form, click `Theme` to open the **Form designer**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/th1.webp" alt="Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form" />
</Frame>

Select from any of the `Featured` or past themes. Previous form designs are saved under under `My themes`.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/th2.webp" alt="Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form" />
</Frame>

## How to customize your chosen theme

Click `Custom theme` to modify visual elements, and tailor the theme to your liking.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/images/the3.png" alt="Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form" />
</Frame>

<Note>
  This feature is available to **Starter** users and above. For more details on pricing, click [here](https://www.fillout.com/pricing).
</Note>

The following are the elements you can customize in the left-hand panel:

- **Background** color (for [container over background layout](/help/layout))
- **Questions background** color
- **Primary** color of buttons and accents throughout the form
- **Questions** text color
- **Answers** text color
- **Font** style

<Check>
  **Good to know:** You can choose from any Google font or [upload your own font](/help/form-fonts).
</Check>

Highlight text to change its font size, style, or color.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/th4.webp" alt="Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form" />
</Frame>

You can also add and adjust:

- [**Logo**](/help/logo) for a professional and polished look
- **Position** or layout of your form
- **Image** uploaded or linked to the form
- **Brightness** of the image
- **Questions** button size
- **Alignment** of questions
