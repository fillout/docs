## How to add a video

First, drag the `Video` field onto your form.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/images/image-28.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>

Click the `settings icon` to open the right-hand panel. Paste the **Video URL** of the video you want to display. Adjust the **Max height** as needed.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/images/image-29.png" alt="Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form" />
</Frame>

## Let respondents upload a video

To collect video submissions from your respondents, simply click the `File uploader` field in the left-hand panel.
