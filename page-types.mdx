## How to add a page

By default, new forms in Fillout are **single-page forms** with a single **Page** and an **Ending** page, which you can see at the bottom of the form.

To create **multi-page forms**, click `+ Add page` and select your [page type](https://www.notion.so/Page-types-ac31ca2695c04b3b9881e7d62b62302c?pvs=21).

![9 Web](/images/9.webp)

## Copy and paste a page

Click `⋮` or the vertical ellipsis next to the name of the page followed by `Copy`. Then, paste anywhere using `⌘ + V` or `Ctrl + V`.

![10 Web](/images/10.webp)

Alternatively, you may also click `Duplicate`.

## Change the order of pages

You can arrange the order pages are shown to respondents through [**page logic**](https://www.notion.so/Page-logic-82c7a7c7c8174289b27f841f5869743d?pvs=21) in the lower right corner.

![11 Web](/images/11.webp)
